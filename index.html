  • School Vision: TO NURTURE AND PREPARE OUT GOING CITIZENS Core Values:
    1. Respect: We value respect for ourselves, others, and our environment.
    2. Integrity: We act with honesty, transparency, and accountability in all our interactions.
    3. Empathy: We strive to understand and appreciate the perspectives and feelings of others.

    6. Inclusivity: We celebrate diversity, promote equality, and provide opportunities for all students to succeed.
    7. Responsibility: We take ownership of our actions, our learning, and our community.
    8. Kindness: We cultivate a culture of kindness, compassion, and empathy towards others.
    9. Excellence: We strive for excellence in all aspects of school life, from academics to extracurricular activities.
    10. Community: We value the importance of building strong relationships within our school community.
