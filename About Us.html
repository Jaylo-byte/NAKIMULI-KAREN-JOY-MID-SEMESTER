History of the School

Kazo Summit was founded in 2010 by a group of dedicated educators who sought to provide a high-quality education to the children of Kampala. Since its humble beginnings, the school has grown to become one of the leading primary schools in the region. Our school has a strong reputation for academic excellence, innovative teaching methods, and a commitment to community service.

    Kazo Summit is proud to offer a range of modern facilities that provide a safe and supportive learning environment for our students. Some of our facilities include:

    • Classrooms:
      • Spacious and well-equipped classrooms with interactive whiteboards and state-of-the-art technology.
    • Library:
      • A well-stocked library with a wide range of books, journals, and digital resources.
    • Computer Lab:
      • A modern computer lab with internet access and the latest software applications.
    • Playground:
      • A safe and well-maintained playground with a range of equipment and facilities for outdoor play.
    • Cafeteria:
      • A clean and well-maintained cafeteria that provides healthy and nutritious meals for our students.
